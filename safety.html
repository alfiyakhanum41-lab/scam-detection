Crime Prediction – Safety Measures

Stay Safe, Stay Aware

Crime prediction helps identify high-risk zones and times. These safety measures empower you to protect yourself and your community.

🏠 Home & Property Safety

• Install motion lights and CCTV cameras.
• Lock windows and doors even during daytime.
• Avoid telling strangers when you're away.
• Keep valuables out of visible areas.
• Use AI-based home monitoring apps.

🚶 Personal Safety

• Stay alert in isolated areas.
• Avoid earbuds at full volume while walking.
• Carry a whistle or an SOS alert device.
• Inform someone when traveling alone.
• Use trusted public or shared transport.

📱 Digital Protection

• Use strong, unique passwords.
• Beware of fraudulent websites and links.
• Enable two-factor authentication everywhere.
• Avoid sharing personal location openly.
• Keep mobile security apps updated.

🚗 Travel & Navigation Safety

• Check predicted crime hotspots on the map.
• Use well-lit routes during night travel.
• Verify taxi/ride driver credentials.
• Keep emergency contacts accessible.
• Avoid displaying expensive items in public.

⚡ AI-Assisted Crime Prevention

• Use your city’s crime prediction alerts.
• Review daily risk ratings for your area.
• Report suspicious activity via apps.
• Follow real-time safety notifications.
• Use smart wearables for emergency alerts.

🚨 Emergency Preparedness

• Memorize key emergency numbers.
• Learn self-defense basics.
• Keep a safety kit in home & car.
• Stay calm and follow emergency protocols.
• Know the nearest police & medical services.
